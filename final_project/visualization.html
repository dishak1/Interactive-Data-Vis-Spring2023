Stacked Bar Graph of Education Attained by Women who gave Birth

Interpretation: From the above graph, we can conclude that United States has a very mixed population when it comes to the Education factor. Most of the states have alot of womens holding more than a high school degree. We can see that North Dakota tops the list. However, the fact that was mentioned in the information section, regarding the correlation between higher education and pregnancy rate, does not reflect completely in this graph. By comparing the two graphs, we can visualize that the rate of women holding post graduate degree is higher that the women under the age group of 35-50. This means that women are getting pregnant before they attain post-graduate degrees. In other words, women do choose to study after having a baby. Apart from this, we can see that most of the women who got pregnant in their teenage, does not study further.
